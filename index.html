About Us

Ekaya Corporation based in Gujarat, India, we are a quality-focused trading and manufacturing company. Our strong ties with aroma creators in India enable us to provide distinctive offerings, enhancing your product.

We are committed supplier of premium flavor and fragrance chemicals, recognized for our reliability and diverse product range that elevates taste and scent profiles across various applications.

Specializing in delivering a variety of flavor and fragrance chemicals to industries such as food & beverage, cosmetics, and personal care, we are here to cater to your specific needs.
